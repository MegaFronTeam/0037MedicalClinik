Welcome To Our Hospital!

We offer extensive medical procedures to outbound and inbound patients.

In 16 hospitals, institutes and interdisciplinary centers of our clinic, we can offer you the modern diagnostic methods, the latest treatment methods and individual care in the inpatient and outpatient area.

We have 658 hospital beds and more than 1,900 employees of medical care, management and service; and more than 60,000 patients use our services every year. Our range of services includes not only round-the-clock ambulance in the Central Emergency Department, but also highly qualified procedures in selected specialized departments.
